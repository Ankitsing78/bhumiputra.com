Easy Returns & Replacement Policy

At Bhumiputra, we stand behind our products. Our factory-direct model guarantees transparent support.

1. 10-Day Worry-Free Returns

If you're not satisfied with your purchase or if the seat/part doesn't fit, you can return it within 10 days of delivery. The item must be unused, unmounted, and in its original packaging. Contact us via WhatsApp or call our support line to schedule a pick-up.

2. Replacement of Defective Items

If you receive an item with transport damage or manufacturing flaws, we will issue a direct replacement immediately at no extra cost to you. Please share photos or videos of the defect via WhatsApp within 48 hours of delivery.

3. Refund Processing

Once your returned item is inspected at our factory warehouse, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original bank account or via UPI. For COD orders, we will request your bank details or UPI ID for transfer.

4. Warranty Coverage

All Bhumiputra products carry a standard 1-Year Structural Warranty covering metal frame welds, suspension mechanisms, and exhaust structural integrity. Foam cores and fabric covers are covered for 6 months against premature sag or stitching wear under regular usage conditions.

Buy factory-direct with absolute confidence. We maintain a zero-friction replacement program for all our institutional and commercial vehicle customers. If a fitment issue arises, we'll swap it or refund you, no arguments.
